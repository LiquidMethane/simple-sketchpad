Freehand line: press and drag to draw freehand lines
straight line: press and drag to draw straight lines
Rectangle: press and drag to draw rectange, hold shift to make it square
Ellipse: press and drag to draw ellipse, hold shift to make it circle
Polygon: press and drad to draw a side, double-click any time to finish (may require some skill)
Cut: select any object by pressing on it, and go to Edit->Cut
Copy: select any object by pressing on it, and go to Edit->Copy
paste: Edit->paste
Undo: Edit->Undo
Redo: Edit->Redo
Save: File->Save
Load: File->Load
